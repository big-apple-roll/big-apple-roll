# Millennium Hotel Broadway Times Square

For the second time in a row in BAR’s 22 year history, our host hotel is in iconic Times Square! Located on [W 44th Street](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Jycx8FCa2tkR1gJ19), just off Broadway, the Millennium Hotel Times Square puts you in the heart of Manhattan, a short stroll (or roll) from Bryant and Central Parks, the Chrysler and Empire State Buildings, the Theater District, and myriad transportation services (including to JFK, LaGuardia, and Newark airports).
