The Big Apple Roll Friday Night Skate (FNS) is one not to miss! Famous for its high energy, the Friday Night Skate is as euphoric as it is fun, hitting all of the famous Manhattan hot spots: Flatiron, 1st Ave Tunnel, Broadway, Central Park, Midtown, and of course, Times Square. Dress up, bring your lights, bring your friends… you never know what will happen on BAR’s Friday Night Skate.
